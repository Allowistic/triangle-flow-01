Triangle Flow 01

Touch to spawn new triangles.
Move to guide the living field.

by @allowistic

Move your mouse or finger — triangles gently repel and return.
Touch or click anywhere to birth small new triangles that float and fade.
A living, responsive field in constant gentle becoming.

Triangles breathe and drift. Red-to-plum glows pulse at their edges while turquoise melts into purple within. Delicate inner triangles hold quiet light. Now, every touch brings new life — small triangles spawn, drift upward, and dissolve back into the field.

This is an evolving piece. Each iteration deepens the conversation between code, touch, and presence. The work remains open — never finished, always allowing.
