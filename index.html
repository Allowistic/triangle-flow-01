Triangle Flow 01

An interactive computational artwork exploring gentle flow, presence, and allowing.

by @allowistic

Move your mouse or finger across the canvas.
The triangles respond with gentle repulsion and organic return.
Tap or click anywhere to energize the flow.

A living field of overlapping triangles. Outer edges glow from vivid red into deep plum. Bodies shift through turquoise into rich purple. Delicate inner triangles catch and hold light.

Created in p5.js as part of ongoing explorations in computational aesthetics, flow states, and the art of allowing.

Part of the Allowistic Alcove series — spaces for creative flow and presence.

“Creativity flows in the waves of now.”
