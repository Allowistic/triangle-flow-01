Triangle Flow 01

Touch to birth flowing forms.
Watch them drift on living currents.

by @allowistic

Move your mouse or finger — large triangles gently repel and return home.
Touch or click anywhere to spawn small triangles that now drift on organic Perlin flow fields.
Touch near a large triangle to trigger recursive subdivision — new child forms are released into the current.

Large triangles hold steady presence with red-to-plum glowing edges and turquoise-to-purple fills, their inner triangles quietly luminous. Blue shadows give them weight and depth.

Now the field has become more alive: every touch releases small triangles that move according to smooth Perlin noise flow fields — organic, wave-like currents rather than mechanical paths. Touching near a main form can trigger recursive subdivision, birthing child triangles that join the flowing ecosystem.

This is an evolving generative artwork. Structure and randomness dance together. The piece remains open — always allowing new becoming.
